How I work

Each film starts with a script or treatment, usually handwritten. Production is lean — small crews, natural light where possible, real locations. Post is done in-house.

01 / DEVELOPMENT

Script

Everything starts longhand. First draft is for finding out what it's about. The second is for structure.

02 / PRE-PRODUCTION

Prep

Location recce, casting, shot list. I storyboard the emotional beats, not every angle.

03 / PRODUCTION

Shoot

Skeleton crew. Usually 2–3 days. Handheld where it helps, locked-off where stillness earns it.

04 / POST

Edit & grade

Edited in Premiere. Graded in DaVinci. Sound mixed at home — the room is quiet enough.
