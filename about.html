About me

Writer, director, Newcastle.

I grew up watching films at the cinema all the time and fell in love with the feeling of seeing a movie on the big screen at an early age. I have worked on screenplays and small recording work for years but am now finally deciding to take the plunge and develop my own stories into real life.

I grew up in Newcastle which has a very rich film culture and history. The town and the stories it created for me growing is a big influence in my writing and I believe that reflects in my work

I write everything longhand first. The first draft is for finding out what the story is actually about. The second is for structure. By the third I'm usually cutting the scenes I was most proud of. That's when it gets good.

I love films from all genres, but I tend to lean more into the horror and romance genre.

I am open to collaboration from everyone in the film industry and if you live in or around the Newcastle area, then I am happy to get onboard with any projects you would like to create.
