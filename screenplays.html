The Fog at Hartley

Drama · Feature · 4 drafts

Draft 1

Eleanor hasn't left the farmhouse in six years. When her estranged son arrives unannounced, the coastal fog that's been creeping in each morning suddenly has company. A slow-burn drama about memory, inheritance, and the things we leave unsaid until it's almost too late.

Saltburn Road

Drama · Short (22 min) · 3 drafts

Draft 1

A teenage girl visits her grandmother every Sunday. One morning the grandmother doesn't answer the door, and the girl must decide how long to wait on the step. A single-location short about the patience we owe each other.

Between Shifts

Documentary · Short (15 min) · 2 drafts

Draft 1

Three NHS nurses. Four nights. A documentary treatment exploring the hours between the end of a shift and the beginning of sleep — what people do with the strange half-time of a life lived on rotation.

Draft progression

Treatment
Final
Treatment — Aug 2024 Initial pitch document. Structure loose — three acts map to three nurses. Tightening required once access is confirmed.
BETWEEN SHIFTS A documentary treatment The night shift ends at 07:30. By 07:45 the car park is clearing. By 08:00, SARAH is on the A19, radio off, window cracked. She won't sleep until noon. She doesn't know what to do with those hours. Neither do we. That's the film.
